Project: US Prison Data

Originally I wanted to compare global prison data, but due to differences in reporting and availability of data (particularly around recidivisim), I had to confine my comparisons to the US. While there still are differences in reporting (more info at the end of this page), I was able to make comparisons much easier than on a global scale.

Chart 1:

My first chart shows most of the data I will be using in my analysis clearly on a map, where each field can be selected to view.

Chart 2:

My second chart plots each state's recidivism rate against the spending per prisoner, with the bubbles indicating the prisoners per 100,000.
